Faculty and Staff:

Brigish Institute of Technology is supported by a
team of dedicated faculty members and professional staff
committed to academic excellence and student success. Our
educators bring extensive knowledge, industry experience,
and a passion for teaching, ensuring that students receive
a high-quality education grounded in both theory and practical application.
The administrative and support staff work diligently to provide
efficient services, maintain a welcoming campus environment,
and assist students throughout their academic journey. Together,
our faculty and staff strive to foster innovation, integrity,
collaboration, and lifelong learning within the Brigish Institute
of Technology community.
